WHERE WE CAME FROM

For professional uniform work apparel, wear Horace Small Uniforms. This workwear brand focuses on durable shirts, pants, and outerwear for the law enforcement and security industries. Series include the Sentry Plus, Sentinel, and New Generation. Sentry Plus shirts and pants are professional apparel with features like durable twill fabric, moisture management, permanent soil release, and Cool-Flex waistbands. There are styles and sizes for men and women. Sheriff trousers are comfortable for all day wear and hold-up to hard work.

Sentinel basic security shirts have front chest pockets, durable press with soil release fabric, and a two-piece lined and banded collar with collar stays. Horace Small Uniform outerwear includes jackets and vests. They help you stay warm and protected from the elements. Breathable waterproof shells keep you comfortable. Add your name or logo to Horace Small Uniforms with screen print, vinyl heat press or embroidery.
