Sales Policy

A1 Uniforms applies sales policies to customers who have purchased at Shop. Applied when customers buy goods both on website khotrangtadi.com and on fanpage system. Specifically, the following applies:

1. Terms of purchase

You can buy any designer fashion products at our website A1uniformsshop888@gmail.com, after a successful order, the shop's staff will contact and reconfirm your order.

Customers can order goods on our sales system in many different forms such as: ordering directly through the website, ordering via Fanpage Uniform A1 - Fashion Design or calling directly to the phone number. Phone 058 581 8883 to order.

In addition, you can check the goods comfortably before paying. Provided video, real pictures for customer reference.

2. Payment Terms

2.1. Payment by bank transfer

After placing an order on the webiste, you can use any bank account to pay through the Internet Banking feature.

Transfer information please contact us to be provided.

2.2. Payment on delivery (COD)

If the customer chooses the payment method after receiving the goods, after receiving the goods, please pay cash to the Shipper to complete the purchase procedure at Shop.

3. Regulations on exchange and return of goods

Customers who buy goods at TADI can return, exchange and refund in the following cases:

  • Support to change the size when the customer does not fit (no change of model).
  • Defective products due to the manufacturer: customers return without any fee.
  • Products with wrong materials, shapes, images, descriptions on the website: customers can exchange for products of the same type or other products of equal or greater value than the purchased product.

4. Regulations on transportation

Shipping time is from 2 to 5 days depending on the geographical distance between you and Shop.
